The Problem

What had started as a small problem eventually became a big problem. It took me almost a week to get the faulty TR-59x, diagnose it, and replace it. During that time however my velocity had increased massively. I guess a week of accelerating acceleration will do that. By the time I had everything fixed I was traveling nearly ½ the speed of light!
